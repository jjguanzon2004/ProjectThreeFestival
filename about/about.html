About Us

For decades, Asian Americans have struggled to gain a foothold in film, music, and entertainment. From the early depcitions of the "Fu Man Chu" and "Dragon Lady" to the ongoing fight for authentic representation, the systemic barriers have limited opportunities and perpetuated harmful attitudes toward the AAPI community. That stops here.


We aim to undo the long history of dicrimination, misrepresentation, and exclusion of Asian Americans in the media. At its core, the Asian Voices Rising Festival is a safe place for Asian Americans to be heard, acknowledged, and accepted. As we bring together Asian artists from film, music, literature, dance, and entertainment, we hope these stories resonate with the masses. With the collective power of Asian artists, we believe we can redefine what it means to Asian American in today's world.

Our Partner

We're glad to announce our partner for this event, the Kearny Street Workshop! The KSW is a local organization in San Francisco dedicated to showing AAPI talent across mulitidisciplinary arts. Together with the Kearny Street Workshop, we are committed to bringing you a memorable event that prommotes AAPI diversity.
